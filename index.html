⚡ Reactive Strategy

"Wait and fix" — Respond after the risk occurs

  • Fire-fighting approach
  • Higher cost of resolution
  • Crisis management mode

🛡️ Proactive Strategy

"Plan ahead" — Identify and mitigate before occurrence

  • Risk identification early
  • Risk projection & refinement
  • RMMM plan creation
